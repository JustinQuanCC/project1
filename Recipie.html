Hainanese Chicken

  • Time: 45-60 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Whole Chicken (Giblets removed) 3 Ibs
Kosher Salt 1/4 Cup
Peeled Fresh Ginger 1/4-inch slices(6 mm)
Fresh Scallions 1 Fresh Bundle
Cold Water 1 gal (more when needed)
Sesame Oil 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Clean the chicken
Start by rubbing it with a handful of kosher salt.
This will get rid of any loose skin.
Rinse the chicken inside and out.
Pat dry with paper towels.

Step 2: Prep the chicken
Start by removing any excess fat from the chicken and set this aside for later.

Step 3: Season the Chicken
Generously season the chicken with salt.
Stuff the chicken cavity with ginger slices and scallions.

Step 4: Cooking the chicken
Place the chicken in a large stock pot and cover with cold water by 1 inch.
Salt the water to taste.

Step 5: Cooking the chicken
Bring the chicken to a boil over high heat before immediately reducing the heat to slow to maintain a simmer.
Cover and cook for about 30 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165F.
Remove the pot from the heat.

Step 6: Finishing the chicken
Remove the chicken from the pot and reserve the poaching liquid if you want to make Hainanese Rice.
Transfer chicken to an ice bath to stop cooking to prevent overcooked chicken.
Dicard the Ginger and Scallions.
Rub the chicken with sesame oil to prevent it from drying out.

From this point forwards, the chicken is done and can be eaten, but it is recommended to also make Hainanese Rice to accompany the chicken.

Butter Chicken Curry

  • Time: 30-45 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breasts (cubed) 1 Ibs
Tandoori Masala Spice Mix 4 tablespoons
Natural Plain Yogurt (Plain) 1 Cup
Ghee or Vegetable Oil 2 tablespoons
Medium Onion (Chopped) 1 Onion
Garlic (minced) 3 Cloves
Ginger Paste or Grated Ginger 1 tablespoon
Coriander 1 tablespoon
Turmeric 1/2 teaspoon
Tomato Paste 2 tablespoons
Water 1/2 Cup
Whipping cream/double cream 1/2 Cup
Fresh Cilantro (chopped) 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Marinade the chicken
In a medium bowl, mix the yogurt with the tandoori masala spice mix and add the chicken.
Marinade for at least 3 hours but overnight is recommended.

Step 2: Cook the aeromatics
In a deep pan, heat the ghee or oil and fry the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger together over low heat for 10 minutes.
This helps bring out the flavors and smells of said ingredients.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken
Add the chicken with the marinade to the pan, together with coriander, tumeric, tomato paste, and water.
Stir to combine and turn up the heat to medium to bring the pan to a boil.
Once it reaches a boil, lower the heat and simmer for 20 minutes uncovered.

Step 4: Add more flavors to the curry
Add the double cream and stir to combine.
Cook uncovered for 10 minutes or until the sauce is to the desired thickness and consistancy
Salt to taste.

Step 5: Serve with fresh cilantro and basmati rice
